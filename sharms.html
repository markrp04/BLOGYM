Sharms (Shoulders & Arms) Workout

Overhead Press Builds strong shoulders and engages triceps. 4 sets 10–12 reps Controlled pressing
Lateral Raises Isolation for side delts, creates width. 3 sets 12–15 reps Light weight, strict form
Arnold Press Engages all 3 heads of the shoulders with rotation. 3 sets 10–12 reps Rotate palms while pressing
Barbell Bicep Curls Classic mass builder for biceps. 4 sets 12, 10, 8, 6 reps Progressively increase weight
Hammer Curls Hits brachialis and forearms for thickness. 3 sets 12 reps Neutral grip
Skull Crushers Great isolation move for triceps long head. 3 sets 10–12 reps EZ bar or dumbbells
Tricep Dips Builds triceps mass and strength. 3 sets 12 reps Bodyweight or weighted
Concentration Curls Strict isolation for peak biceps contraction. 3 sets 12 reps each arm Slow & controlled
