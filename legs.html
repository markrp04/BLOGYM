Leg Exercise List

Squats The king of leg exercises – targets quads, glutes, and hamstrings. 4 sets 12, 10, 8, 6 reps Progressive overload with barbell or dumbbells
Lunges Great unilateral movement for balance, stability, and quads/glutes. 3 sets 12 reps each leg Bodyweight or dumbbells
Leg Press Mass builder that isolates quads and glutes safely. 4 sets 12, 10, 8, 6 reps Increase weight each set
Romanian Deadlifts Targets hamstrings and glutes with a deep stretch. 4 sets 10 reps Focus on controlled motion
Leg Extensions Isolation exercise for quad definition. 3 sets 12–15 reps Light to moderate weight
Hamstring Curls Isolates hamstrings and prevents muscle imbalance. 3 sets 12–15 reps Controlled motion
Bulgarian Split Squats Powerful unilateral exercise for quads and glutes. 3 sets 12 reps each leg Dumbbells optional
Step-Ups Great for functional strength and athletic performance. 3 sets 12 reps each leg Use a bench or platform
Calf Raises Builds calf strength and definition. 4 sets 15–20 reps Bodyweight or weighted
Glute Bridges Targets glutes and hamstrings; great finisher. 3 sets 15 reps Bodyweight or barbell
